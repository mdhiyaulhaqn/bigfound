ABOUT US

International Relations Project Universitas Indonesia (IR Project UI) is the biggest annual event, distinctively, held by Himpunan Mahasiswa Ilmu Hubungan Internasional Fakultas Ilmu Sosial dan Ilmu Politik (HMHI FISIP UI). IR Project is an expansion of Indonesia Foreign Policy Review which had been held several years before. IR Project consisted of three main events: IFPR Journal Launching, Diplomatic Gala Dinner, and HI-Day.

THIS YEAR'S THEME

The Changing Patterns of Diplomacy:
How is Indonesia’s Position?
This year, International Relations has been established for 100 years. This indicates that for 100 years, International Relations has also contributed to the development of the world. In this regard, Indonesia as a country, also takes its own part in discussion of this knowledge. Emerging as an independent country after World War II, Indonesia, has an interesting diplomacy that helps the country to get a spotlight in this field.
